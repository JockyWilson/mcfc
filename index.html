Welcome to Music Choice Football Club

Music Choice have been hacking their way around the Music Association Leagues since 2001. A league title, 7 European tours and over a hundred players later the team is still going strong. Season 2007/08 was not a vintage year (more Blue Nun than Mouton Rothschild '61) but The Gaffer is already regrouping and planning an assault on the Division 1 title.

Season 2008/09

The fixtures for the new season have been published and The Gaffer has made over half a dozen premium signings to strengthen the squad. He was in confident mood when we spoke to him at the team's pre-season training camp just off junction 2 of the M45, "Last year I was staring down the barrel into the abyss I had dug myself into using my 28" bulldog evergreen digging spade, I'm now out of the abyss and looking to fill it in".
Music Choice FC are sponsored by The Falmouth Garden Centre
