Football shirts are important, The Gaffer knows this, and to date the team have worn 5 different kits. Coveted for their aphrodisiac qualities many of these shirts have been lost, stolen or handed to bemused Eastern Europeans over the years.

Kit Number 1 : Red

First up was a brief flirtation with the Nike brand. This was the only shirt that actually had Music Choice emblazoned across the front and probably the only kit that Music Choice knowingly paid for. Undoubtedly the shiniest of all the Music Choice shirts.
shirt : red with white trim
shorts : navy blue
socks : navy blue
logos : music choice and small target across front
goalkeeper's top : blue
shirt numbers : no

Kit Number 2 : Navy Blue

The love affair with Kappa starts here, a classic navy shirt as worn by the famous touring party of 2003. A second, low quality batch of these shirts quickly came into circulation to meet demand but these tops are still rarer than a Hughesie clean sheet.
shirt : navy blue
shorts : white
socks : navy blue
logos : large target across front, music choice across on back
goalkeeper's top : black
shirt numbers : yes

Kit Number 3 : AS Roma

Over 3 years of tradition were cast aside when The Choice opted to wear the replica shirt of another team when they set sail for Krakow in 2004, the result was a job lot of Roma tops. Fans of MCFC will forever associate this kit with the infamous defeat at the hands of FC Taban in Poland.
shirt : maroon red
shorts : white
socks : navy blue
logos : none
goalkeeper's top : black
shirt numbers : no

Kit Number 4 : Black

When Henning was handed a black shirt and told we were invading Slovakia in 2005 his chest swelled with pride, he certainly gave more than his usual 110% on that tour. The shirts didn't fit if you were over 5ft 6 but otherwise this was a snazzy ensemble.
shirt : black with white trim
shorts : white
socks : white
logos : music choice across the back
goalkeeper's top : red
shirt numbers : yes

Kit Number 5 : White

This white Puma kit was proudly sponsored by Black Box Market Research, offering research and business solutions throughout the UK and the rest of Europe. After thrashing out a groundbreaking 'Points + league goals - league goals conceded = cash payable' sponsorship deal, the club currently owe Black Box in the region of Â£40.
shirt : white
shorts : white
socks : white
logos : Black Box Market Research across the front
goalkeeper's top : aqua marine
shirt numbers : yes

Kit Number 6 : Black (again)

It was back in black for Sofia 2008 as The Gaffer returned to the tried and trusted classic that first struck fear into the opposition 3 years ago.
The kit is modelled here by MCFC's all time leading tour goalscorer,
Tobias 'The Kaiser' Philipps.
